Baby shop

We have a wide selection of quality baby supplies for rent. Pick-up and return 24/7. Our brands include Birtax, Stokke and Hokka, for example. We are constantly updating our product selection and if the product you are looking for cannot be found in our selection, please contact us. We will inspect and clean all our products before the next rental. When you rent a product, you will receive an order confirmation email with the product's location and pick-up information after payment. You can pick up the products whenever you want (24h). The rental day always starts at 10 am, ending on the last rental day at 10 am. If you want a user guide, want to get to know the product or want to rent directly on site, please contact us by calling. You can also request a package offer from us.
